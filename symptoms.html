Symptoms

Do you know what some OCD symptoms are?

Repetitive behaviour

Repeating actions until they are "just right" or starting things over again.

Rule-driven

Maintaining a rigid system that dictates your behaviour.

Resistant to change

Find it difficult/distressing when things don't go as you expected or when things change.

Excessive checking

Excessive checking of things, such as ensuring doors are locked multiple times, having a difficulty tolerating uncertainty.

Uncontrollable intrusive thoughts

Unwanted thoughts, images or urges that occur over and over again.

Time-consuming tasks

Spending too much time on routine tasks (eg., washing hands, leaving the house).

Excessive worries

Always checking on things and having fears about bad things happening or doing something wrong.

Complications

Certain complications can arise from OCD.

These may include...

Excessive time spent engaging in ritualistic behaviours

Health issues, such as contact dermatitis from frequent hand-washing

Troubled relationships

Suicidal thoughts and behaviour

Difficulty attending work, school or social activities!
